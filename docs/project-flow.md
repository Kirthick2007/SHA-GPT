# ClaimShield AI Project Flow

## Demo Flow

1. Open dashboard.
2. Show total claims and suspicious claims.
3. Open claim detection page.
4. Submit or select a claim.
5. Show risk score, decision, and suspicious reasons.
6. Open provider risk page.
7. Show providers with high claim anomalies.
8. Open audit log page.
9. Show tamper-evident claim decision history.

## Risk Levels

- Low Risk: claim can move faster for payout.
- Medium Risk: claim needs manual review.
- High Risk: claim should be investigated before payout.

## Special Features

- Real-time fraud risk scoring.
- Provider anomaly detection.
- Explainable suspicious reasons.
- Tamper-evident audit log.
